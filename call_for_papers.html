Call For Papers

The Haryana Police Journal covers analysis, reviews, and insights into the criminal justice system, especial on police sciences, and includes aspects of professional development. If you want to contribute an article or a book review, for the current issue of the journal, please use APA (American Psychological Association, https://apastyle.apa.org/ ) style for writing which is most commonly used to cite sources within the social sciences.

Also include an abstract. You may include possible policy or field level implications of your research and future work you see connected with your findings. Please send your article to haryanajournal[at]gmail[dot]com in MS Word format. In case of any clarification, please feel free to contact the editor at hanif[dot]qureshi[at]gov[dot]in.

If you wish to be included as an independent reviewer for the journal, we are happy to do that. Please send a mail to the editor and enclose a brief CV.
